How to Use

Follow these steps:

  1. Open Youtube Video
  2. Copy the YouTube Video url

  3. Paste Video URL in Input Field


  4. After pasting then click on Search Keywords

  5. After clicking on Search Keywords button, keywords/tags will be shown like below

  6. For copying tags, click on Copy Tags

  7. Now tags are copied to clipboard, you can use it anywhere
